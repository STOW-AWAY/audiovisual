AUDIOVISUAL

This is an illustration project concerned with the creation of visual accompaniment for musical collections. We believe that just as music can accompany visual art, so can visual art reinforce musical pieces. We each chose a collection to present. The visitor is Invited to embrace the combined sensory experience.
Please, select one.

New Skin for the Old Ceremony is the fourth studio album by canadian poet and musician Leonard Cohen. Deviating from the raw sound of previous releases, 'New Skin' features varied instrumentation and a denser soundscape.

ART BY Lee R. Pen, watercolour and oil on paper. Freehand lettering. 2023.

Spring Awakening is a stage musical based on the 1891 German play of the same name. It is a coming-of-age story, focusing on a group of teenagers facing the twists and turns of adolescent sexuality in conservtice 19th-century Germany.

ART BY Hila W. Pencil and watercolour on paper, edited digitally. 2023.

NARRATIVE

Traditionally, it was thought that the still image's lack of a trivially apparent temporal element means narrative is alien to it. However, with modern understanding of intertextuality and audience-side authorshup, it becomes clear that this conception is myopic and limiting.
We wished to allow music and art to commune with one another.
